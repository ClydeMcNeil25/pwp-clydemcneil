Milestone Two

Content Strategy

My site will be one page, with the mobile first design concept in mind. The ultimate goal and tone of the site in its entirety, is to have a well professionally structured page that focuses on the projects and the clear design concepts in creating this site. Below the following sections will contain:

Hero Image: As soon as the page loads, there will be a hero image of a graphic that I will create with me in the design. Above in the top left corner, I will have my logo and over to the right will be a navigation bar to help the user navigate through the page.

About Me: Just below the hero image section there will be a picture of myself with a description of my professional history, goals, and my passions. The overall tone will be informal and casual. This section will be filled with the following:

"Hello, I'm Clyde I've been in fascination with everything that has to do with technology and art all of my life. I am currently serving in the United States Air Force, going on now for 21 years and quickly approaching retirement. I'd consider myself having the thirst for knowledge and try to be a better version of myself than yesterday. I'm eagerly ready for my next chapter of my life entering into the tech industry. As a freelance graphic designer, I am passionate about solving my clients problems bringing their ideas to life. Lastly, as a web developer, I love bringing in all of my skill sets in creating meaningful products that bring people together."

Portfolio: In this section, I will list my various skill sets and showcase them in a Z pattern design. In each section they will have a thumbnail and once when the user hovers and click on the thumbnail a responsive popout image will show more in depth image for each section. For the "Motion Graphics" and "Audio Production", there will be a responsive popout video for those sections giving a better example of my work. On the side of each section, I will list the tools with the respective hyperlinks to the product page and my roles in creating the products in their respective areas. In the continuation of the portfolio, I will list my soft skills, education, and certifications as seen in the layout below. Ultimately, I am showcasing my skill sets, in the hopes of promoting my passion for my art and web development along with attracting that potential client and/or my future employer.

Contact: After the user type in their name and email contact information, the user will be able to utilize the dropdown box that will contain; employment, graphic design, web development, UX/UI design, video/audio production services, collaborate, and other. I will also list my professional social sites for the user to keep up to date with my next projects and/or to help build my social media presence. Overall, the intent is for the user to have various ways or preferences to stay in touch with me.

Wireframes

The site will be fully responsive for desktop and mobile viewing options. The wireframes that you see below, were created using Figma. After exporting each mobile wireframe from Figma, I then used Adobe Photoshop to group the mobile wireframe images together and added a drop shadow effect using Photoshops' blending tools section. Then I added text to each mobile screen sections for consistency with the overall layouts and documentation.
