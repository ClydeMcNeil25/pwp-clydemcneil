Content Strategy

My site will be one page, with the mobile first design concept in mind, and they will consist of the following sections:

Landing Page: As soon as the page load, there will be a hero image of a graphic that I will create with me in the design. Above in the left corner will be a navigation bar to help the user navigate through the page.

About Me: In this section there will be a picture of myself with a description of my professional history, strengths, and my future goals. The overall tone will be informal and casual. This section will be filled with the following:

    "Hello, I'm Clyde I've been in fascination with everything that has to do with technology and art all of my life. I am currently serving in the United States Air Force for 21 years and upon retirement, I'm eagerly ready for my next chapter of my life entering into the tech industry. As a freelance graphic designer, I am passionate about solving my clients problems bringing their ideas to life. Lastly, as a web developer, I love bringing in all of my skill sets in creating meaningful products that bring people together."

Portfolio: In this section, I will list my various skill sets and showcase them in a Z pattern design. In each section they will have a thumbnail and once when the user clicks on the thumbnail a responsive popout image will show more in depth image for each section. For the "Motion Graphics" and "Audio Production", there will be a responsive popout video for those sections. On the side of each section, I will list the tools with the respective hyperlinks to the product page and my roles in creating the products in their respective areas. In the continuation of the portfolio, I will list my soft skills, education, and certifications as seen in the layouts. Ultimately, I am showcasing my skill sets, in the hopes of promoting my passion for my art and to attract a potential client and/or employer.

Contact: This section will help the user in contacting me depending on the dropdown box that will contain; employment, be in this section as well. I will also list my professional social sites for the user to get in touch with as well.
