Milestone Two

Content Strategy

My site will be one page, with the mobile first design concept in mind, and will consist of the following sections:

Landing Page: As soon as the page load, there will be a full screen image of a graphic that I will create with me in the design. Above in the left corner will be a navigation bar to help the user navigate through the page more so of a scroll link.

About Me: In this section there will be a picture of myself with a description of my professional history, strengths, and my future goals. The overall tone will be informal and casual. The section will be filled with the following:

    Hello, I'm Clyde I've been in fascination with everything that has to do with technology and art all of my life. I am currently serving in the United States Air Force for 21 years and upon retirement, I'm eagerly ready for my next chapter of my life entering into the tech industry. As a freelance graphic artist, I am passionate about solving my clients problems bringing their ideas to life. Lastly, as a web developer, I love bringing in all of my skill sets in creating meaningful products that bring people together.

Portfolio: In this section, I will list my various skill sets and showcase them in a Z pattern design. In each section they will have a thumbnail and once when the user clicks on the thumbnail a responsive popout image will show more in depth each section.

Contact: This section will help the user in contacting me depending on the dropdown box that will be in this section as well. I will also list my professional social sites for the user to get in touch with as well.
