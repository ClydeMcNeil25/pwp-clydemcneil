Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Landing Page: This will have a full screen image of a graphic that I will create. Above in the left corner will be a navigation bar to help the user navigate through the page

About Me: In this section there will be a picture of myself with a description of my professional history, strengths, and my future goals. The overall tone will be informal and casual.

Portfolio: In this section, I will list my various skill sets that I have.

Contact: This section will help the user in contacting me depending on the dropdown box that will be in this section as well. I will also list my professional social sites for the user to get in touch with as well.
