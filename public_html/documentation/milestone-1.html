Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website with a focus on UX/UI and Front-End Web Development.

Audience: The design for this site is intended for a professional audience in mind. The primary audience that this site is targeted for, are startup founders, hiring managers, recruiters, and peers. Additionally, this is also aimed towards individuals or groups that need UX/UI and Web Development services.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is to showcase my work for employment, collaboration, and freelance opportunities. Secondly, this site is to help promote my skill sets and establish an online presence in the UX/UI and Web Development communities. Through the use of my social media links and email contact form, the user will have the ability to connect with me.

Persona

Name: Robert Blevins

Age: 41

Profession: Robert is the CEO of Web Fox, operates fully remote and a full-service marketing agency that is considered a "One-Stop Shop" for brands to get their ideas created, with the "Cradle to the Grave" concept in mind. Usually, brands reach out to Web Fox for, creating webpages, apps, video production, logos, brochures, marketing strategies, and various other graphic design options and services.

Technology:

    Hardware:
    Windows and Mac user. Robert has a custom-built PC from NZXT with an i9-11900KF CPU, RTX 3090 Ti, 64 GB of RAM, and 8 TB of SSD storage. He uses two 32" widescreen displays running at 4K resolution. For his portable on-the-go setup, he uses a MacBook Pro laptop.
    Software:
    Robert and his employees makes good use of the Adobe Suite and Affinity Suite for all of their graphic designs and video productions. For 3D projects, he uses Autodesk Maya, Autodesk 3Ds Max, Blender, and ZBrush.
